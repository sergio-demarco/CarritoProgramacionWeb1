    06-24-2014

    Robert De Niro talks about "The Godfather Part IV"

    According to an official statement made by Paramount Pictures, The Godfather IV "might" be in pre-production stage. Rumor says that many distinguished writers from productions like "The Sopranos" and "American Gangster" are involved in the possible sequel to The Godfather Part III.

    De Niro said via his twitter yesterday that The Godfather III was already a "loose step" in the film series and that the simple thought of making a fourth part was "irreverent".

    "Pacino was the only thing that kept The Godfather III away from box office disaster, so why they (Paramount) should think that another entry without the original actors will succeed?" De Niro said harshly in his twitter yesterday.

    Whether De Niro will be casted for a primary role in the movie is still in talks between Paramount's producers and De Niro's manager, who is still pretty quiet about the whole situation. More updates soon.
